Welcome to CampFirecast!

Here at CampFirecast, we aim to support users in determining whether a campfire is safe to light based on location, date selection and weather conditions. We want our campers to be fire conscious to ensure the safety of themselves, fellow campers and the environment.

To get started, simply type in the city you would like to camp in and select the corresponding state from the drop-down menu provided. Based on your search, a Firecast report will display and inform you if it is safe or not safe to light a fire. Thank you for choosing CampFirecast and happy camping!
