Welcome to Camp FireCast

Here at Camp FireCast, we aim to support users in locating a proper camp site that will allow them to have a fire. We want to make sure campers are fire safe and properly equipped with fire safety to ensure the wellbeing of not only other campers but the surrounding areas. Along with keeping the area fire free. To get started, simply type in the city in which you would like to camp and selct the state to coorespond with the city. Based on your search, we will provide data and suggestions to help you be fire conscious. Thank you!
